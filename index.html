Web of Trust

Telepresent events between Trust, Soft Surplus, and the World Wide Web.

Collaboratively produced by Guild, New Computer Working Group, Other Internet.

Web of Trust is an informal event series which takes its name from the PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) encryption and authentication network. Events will cover a range of topics, self-elected by members and friends.
