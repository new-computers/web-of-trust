World Game

19:00, 28 August 2019

Trust, Kluckstr. 25, 10785 Berlin

Cullen Miller, Susan Ploetz, Sam Hart



Cullen Miller will present the research on world games and war games he has conducted in preperation for an upcoming project. The lecture will discuss the Rand Corporation's wargaming techniques, Buckminster Fuller's World Game, the movement toward whole systems thinking and mereotopology, a relational theory of parts and wholes.

Following the presentation will be a participatory scenerio planning excercise produced in collaboration with Susan Ploetz.
