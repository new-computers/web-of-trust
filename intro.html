Web of Trust Introduction + PGP Keysigning Party

19:00, 10 June 2019

Trust, Kluckstr. 25, 10785 Berlin

Sarah Friend, Cory Levinson, Sam Hart

This intro will include presentations about what constitutes a Web of Trust and a brief history of PGP (Pretty Good Privacy), tracing its origins to the early cypherpunk movement.

Afterward, we will hold a PGP Keysigning Cryptoparty to get experience bootstrapping our own Web of Trust.

What to bring:

  • Picture ID
  • Your Key ID
  • Key type
  • Key HEX fingerprint
  • Key size
  • Pen or pencil
  • And / or your laptop
